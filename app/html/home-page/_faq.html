  • How do you estimate the price for your services?

    We typically estimate the price for our services based on a variety of factors, such as:

    Scope of work: The first thing that we consider when estimating the price for our services is the scope of work required. This includes the specific tasks or services that the client needs, such as designing a new theme, setting up a store, or optimizing an existing site.

    Level of customization: The more customized the work, the higher the price. We  may charge more for complex or highly customized work that requires a lot of development or coding.

    Timeframe: Experts may charge different rates depending on how quickly the client needs the work done. Rush projects often come at a higher price.

    Value-added services: We also offer additional services such as training, support, or ongoing maintenance. These services are often charged separately and can increase the overall cost of the project.x
