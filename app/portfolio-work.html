Monosuit

MONOSUIT is a New York based independent fashion brand known for its comfortable avant-garde street wear since.

The Monosuit team contacted us because they wanted to transfer their online shop from Ecwid to Shopify. Once they selected Shopify as their new platform, they requested that we create a brand new store and design a custom website theme to match their specifications. Monosuit's goal was to have an exceptional design and robust features that could support their business worldwide.

We started the project with a detailed analysis of Monosuit's needs and requirements to understand how to best customize the store for their company.

Analysis of customer needs Competitor analysis Thinking through the UX part Development of the UI part of the design Fixing bugs and errors Testing and launching a website

Our team also took responsibility for migrating all the data from the previous Monosuit site to the new Shopify platform. We made sure that the transition was seamless and that all data was preserved and updated.

To ensure the smooth functioning of the store with different currencies and languages, our team implemented the necessary changes and customizations for the store. We also integrated a payment gateway to provide a convenient checkout experience for Monosuit customers from around the world.

In general, our team provided Monosuit with comprehensive support throughout the entire process of developing and launching a Shopify store. We responded to client requests and solved any problems that arose.

Our team did everything possible to ensure that Monosuit's store would launch successfully and become an effective tool for expanding their business.
